Best dog walking spots in Minneapolis, MN

The Minneapolis Dog Kennel Alternative

Minneapolis and St. Paul, often referred to together as the "Twin Cities" are known for their lakes, riverfronts, and parks, making them the ideal puppy pad for the Minneapolitan pooch. If your pup is of the artsy sort, home dog boarding in Minneapolis offers a number of pet-friendly locations for the creative bunch: the Walker Art Center, Minneapolis Sculpture Garden, and The Weisman Art Museum. While beautiful in the spring and summer months, the weather in Minneapolis is known to get pretty extreme. Local residents in the City of Lakes may wish to take a vacay, to escape the winter frost. Minneapolis pet sitting is the best option for the traveling doggy parent, and DogVacay.com is the most efficient service that connects dog boarding hosts with travelers.


DogVacay's Minneapolis dog boarding hosts are ready to give your furry friend a vacation of their own. From taking Fido for a dog walk at the Minnehaha Off-Leash Recreation Area to playing with your pup during a doggy daycare session, Minneapolis pet sitting professionals and dog lovers will be sure to pamper your four-legged friend like they've never been pampered before.
