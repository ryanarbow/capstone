It's no wonder that San Diego is home of PETCO Park and numerous pet-friendly restaurants within the Downtown, East Village and Gaslamp Quarter. Dog boarding in San Diego is ideal with so many outdoor activities at the San Diegoan's disposal, furry friends and beach buddies are welcome with open arms and paws.
