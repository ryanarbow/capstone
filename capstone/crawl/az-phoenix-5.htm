Best dog walking spots in Phoenix, AZ

The Phoenix Dog Kennel Alternative

With its year-round sun and popular art scene, Phoenix is home to over a million people. Since dogs are considered man's best friend, this can only mean one thing: pups are everywhere! With Petsmart's corporate headquarters in Phoenix, it's no wonder that home dog boarding is everywhere! Living in a desert with a pooch may require a refresher every once in a while, and doggy parents and pet sitters in the Valley of the Sun often want to travel to a location with brisker weather. Phoenix dog boarding is the best option for the traveling dog owner, and DogVacay.com is the most efficient service that connects dog sitters with travelers.


There's no shortage of fun activities for those pet sitting in Phoenix, Arizona's Urban Heart. From taking your pooch for a dog walk at Grovers Basin Dog Park to lounging under the trees at Central Phoenix's Washington Park to offering Fido a dip in the lake at famous Cosmo Dog Park during a doggy daycare session, DogVacay's Phoenix dog boarding hosts are ready to give your pooch a doggy vacation of their own!
