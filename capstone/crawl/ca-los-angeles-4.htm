Home to the poshest of pooches. The energized LA dog can be your Doga partner (Dog + Yoga = Doga), your co-worker in pet-friendly offices, and most of all, your best friend. In Los Angeles, fabulous furry friends are no stranger to the big screen, as Hollywood has had its fair share of celebrity dogs (Lassie, anyone?). Los Angeles dog lovers tend to live hectic lives, so it's no surprise when the time comes for the hard working Angeleno to take a business trip or two. Los Angeles dog boarding is the best option for the traveling doggie parent, and DogVacay.com is the most trusted website for connecting dog owners with the best pet sitting services in Los Angeles.
