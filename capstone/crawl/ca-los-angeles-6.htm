The Los Angeles Dog Kennel Alternative

Home to the poshest of pooches. With celebrities hitting the pet-friendly streets of Beverly Hills, Silver Lake and Santa Monica in their fashionable dog carriers and the temperature rarely going below 75 degrees, Los Angeles is the ideal city for the outdoorsy dog. That's why dog boarding in Los Angeles is becoming increasingly popular. The energized LA dog can be your Doga partner (Dog + Yoga = Doga), your co-worker in pet-friendly offices, and most of all, your best friend. In Los Angeles, fabulous furry friends are no stranger to the big screen, as Hollywood has had its fair share of celebrity dogs (Lassie, anyone?). Los Angeles dog lovers tend to live hectic lives, so it's no surprise when the time comes for the hard working Angeleno to take a business trip or two. Los Angeles dog boarding is the best option for the traveling doggie parent, and DogVacay.com is the most trusted website for connecting dog owners with the best pet sitting services in Los Angeles.


Los Angeles pet sitters provide multiple services: dog boarding for the traveling doggie parent or dog daycare for the 9-5 worker. No matter the occasion, pet sitting in Los Angeles gives pooches a loving home and an experience worth remembering. Whether they are taking your pup for an eclectic walk down Venice Beach or introducing him/her to some canine companions at Runyon Canyon Dog Park, you can rest assure that Fido will hit all of the doggy hot spots Los Angeles has to offer. DogVacay.com helps you choose the best home dog boarding options in Los Angeles based on location, rate, special requirements, and length of stay. By getting photo updates of your dog sent straight to your phone, you can travel with the peace of mind that your dog is enjoying a vacation of their own!
