The Austin Dog Kennel Alternative

Known as the Live Musical Capital of the World, Austin is a dog-friendly town that any pup would enjoy painting red. Its lively music, film, and interactive events and festivals including SXSW, and Austin City Limits, although really exciting, may not be suitable for the sensitive-eared pooch or accommodate pet-friendy needs. That's why DogVacay.com offers a list of trusted and insured Austin dog boarding hosts who are ready to provide your pup with pet services such as dog walking, doggy daycare, and home dog boarding. So while you're rocking out to musical legends at the Old Settler's Music Festival, your dog will be enjoying quality time with other dog lovers and her Austin pet sitting host at Zilker Dog Park, Auditorium Shores or McKinney Falls State Park, enjoying a vacation of their own!
