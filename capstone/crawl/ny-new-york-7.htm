Sophisticated pups are no stranger to the Big Apple. New Yorkers are constantly on the go, so it's no surprise that many of them need to find a trusted and insured New York pet sitting services as they're jet setting around the world. In New York City, home dog boarding is the best option for the traveling doggie parent, and DogVacay.com is the most efficient service that connects pet sitting professionals and dog lovers with travelers.


With dog-friendly local attractions gracing the streets of New York City, dog boarding and pet sitting services are just an arm (er, paw) reach away. Whether they're taking their new furry friend to the Downtown Doghouse on 18th Street for some gourmet dog treats or heading to the Union Sqaure Park dog run for a quick jog, dog boarding hosts in this lively city have an endless supply of options for the best doggy hot spots.
