The Atlanta Dog Kennel Alternative

Atlanta, the birthplace of Coca Cola and Chic-Fil-A, is home to more than just hot weather and good ol' southern hospitality. It's also home to some amazing pet friendly destination spots like Stone Mountain Park. That famous southern hospitality can also be experienced when traveling pet parents need dog boarding in Atlanta or just daily pet friendly outings around the city. With over 100 dog-friendly restaurants, this city gives doggy parents and their four-legged best friends a variety of pet friendly dining options such as 4th & Swift and HobNob in Ansley. As its nickname, Hotlanta, so aptly describes, this city is ideal for taking your dog out during the spring and summer days for some outdoor fun including trips to Piedmont Dog Park or Park Grounds for coffee and lunch with friends while you dogs enjoy its fully fenced in park. But if you're looking to escape the heat during vacation season, DogVacay.com's trusted and affordable Atlanta dog boarding hosts are ready to provide your canine with pet services such as pet sitting, doggy daycare, and home dog boarding. From couch napping to dog walking to dog training, your pooch will come back from his or her Atlanta pet sitting adventure happy and tall wagging!
