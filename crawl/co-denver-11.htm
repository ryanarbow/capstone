The Denver Dog Kennel Alternative

Denverites are no stranger to the multitude of activities the Mile High City has to offer. Home dog boarding in Denver is perfect for the active pup who loves vigorous hikes and romping in freshly fallen snow. From skiing and snowboarding to hiking and camping, Denver is a city filled with outdoor adventures and crisp weather. For those weekends where you need to explore without your pooch, DogVacay.com connects Denver pet sitting professionals and experienced dog lovers with traveling doggy parents.


Whether they're visiting Denver's Barnum Park off-leash dog park or visiting galleries in Denver's spectacular art scenes, Denver dog boarding provides doggy parents with the peace of mind that their best friend is in good hands. And of course, Denver dog boarding hosts love to visit Washington Park (aka "Wash Park") to participate in one of the city's best canine social scenes. With pet services including home dog boarding, dog walking, dog grooming, doggy daycare, and pet sitting, DogVacay.com's Denver dog boarding hosts are ready to give your pooch a vacation to remember!
