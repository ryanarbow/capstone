Home to the poshest of pooches. In Los Angeles, fabulous furry friends are no stranger to the big screen, as Hollywood has had its fair share of celebrity dogs (Lassie, anyone?). Los Angeles dog lovers tend to live hectic lives, so it's no surprise when the time comes for the hard working Angeleno to take a business trip or two.
