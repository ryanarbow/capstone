The Miami Dog Kennel Alternative

With its warm climate and clear-water beaches, Miami, also known as the Gateway to America, is a dog-friendly city filled with dog parks, dog beaches, and, of course, loving doggy parents. But when the sun sets and the shades come off, Miami dog owners enter the infamous Miami nightlife scene and may not be able to find a pet-friendly nightclub. That's why DogVacay offers a trusty list of affordable Miami dog boarding hosts who are ready to provide your pup with pet services such as pet sitting, doggy daycare, and home dog boarding.


With Miami's year-round sunshine and beaming sun, your dog will need the occasional rollick on the beach and DogVacay's dog boarding hosts know just the place. Whether they're taking your pooch to Haulover Beach Park for some beach adventures or Tropical Park to get a refreshing drink from the water fountain, DogVacay's Miami pet sitting hosts are ready to give your pooch a vacation to remember!
