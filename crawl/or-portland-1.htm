The Portland Dog Kennel Alternative

Portland is known for its beautiful landscapes, unique microbreweries and extreme pet-friendliness. Some would say that the dogs in this town are treated better than the people making home dog boarding in Portland a must. Portland is the home of Sellwood Riverfront Park, the ideal puppy playground replete with extensive off-leash areas, and quirky dog events like the Annual Pug Crawl and Doggie Dash. Also, the pet-friendly craze is taking the city by storm and Portland has more dog-friendly pubs and restaurants than any city in the world. Portland also has the highest per capita dog parks (5.7) for every 100,000 residents.


It's no question, the city of Roses is THE place for any four-legged friend and pet parents expect the best home dog boarding options. The Portland granola-pooch is used to being smothered with vegan love from their doggy parents at home, which is why home dog boarding in Portland is the best option for the traveling dog owner.
